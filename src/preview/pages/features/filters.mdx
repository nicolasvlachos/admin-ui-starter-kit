<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

`FilterProvider` owns the active-filter state machine; `FilterLayout`
renders the strip (always-visible facets, the Filters menu for collapsed
ones, active pills, and the Clear filters action). Each `FilterConfig`
declares its `type` (`SEARCH`, `SELECT`, `MULTI_SELECT`, `DATE`, `RANGE`,
`TAGS`, `ASYNC_SELECT`), its `displayConfig.display` (`always` or
`collapsed`), and optional `options`/`fetchOptions`/`validation`.

The example below mounts a search facet, a multi-select status facet, and
an async-select customer facet wired to the in-repo mock fetcher in
`src/preview/_mocks/fetchers.ts` (~280ms simulated delay).
