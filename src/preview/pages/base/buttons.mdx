<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

`BaseButton` is the foundational primitive — eight semantic `variant`s (dark,
primary, secondary, error, warning, success, light, action) crossed with three
`buttonStyle`s (solid, outline, ghost) and four sizes (xs, sm, default, lg)
plus four icon-only sizes. `Button` layers on optional `loading`, `withTooltip`,
and `href` so a single component covers most call sites. The specialty buttons
are pre-styled wrappers for common patterns.
