<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

The `forms/` folder ships the canonical form-row wrapper (`FormField` /
`ControlledFormField`) plus every input primitive — `Input`, `Textarea`,
`Select`, numeric/money/dimension/weight inputs, choice groups, date & time
pickers, sliders, tag/phone/coordinates inputs, three repeater families, and
the localized field set. Most fields are framework-free and accept
value/onChange directly. The repeater and localized-* families opt into
`react-hook-form` — that coupling is intentional and contained to those files.

Pair any field with `FormField` (or `ControlledFormField` for RHF) to inherit
the standard label / required asterisk / error / hint rhythm. Pass `invalid`
to the inner field to flip the destructive border + ring.

</Section>
