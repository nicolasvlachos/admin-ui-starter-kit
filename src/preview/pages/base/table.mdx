<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

`DataTable<T>` wraps `@tanstack/react-table` with our typography, density,
and surface tokens. Pass `columns` (typed against your row shape) and `data`,
optionally enable `sorting`, supply `rowActions`, and tune `size` (xs / sm /
base) and `surface` (card / glass / flat). Pair with `Pagination` for the
canonical Laravel-paginator shape.
