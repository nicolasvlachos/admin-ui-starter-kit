<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

`DatePicker` covers all three calendar modes (`single`, `range`, `multiple`)
behind one component — pass `mode` and a matching `value` / `onChange` shape.
`MonthYearPicker` is the compact month + year combo for filters and reports.
Both honour `useDatesConfig()` for locale and date format.
