<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

`SmartCard` is the canonical card surface â€” every dashboard tile, settings
panel, or KPI block runs through it. Three `surface` chromes (`card`, `flat`,
`framed`), three padding scales, optional `headerStart` / `headerEnd` /
`headerAction` slots, action menus, alert bands, footer text or `footerSlot`,
and an `expandable` body for long content.
