<Section title="Overview" id="overview">

The typography layer is intentionally tiny. `Heading` (h1–h6, optional
`subHeading`), `Text` (six sizes, seven types, four weights, optional `asHTML`),
`Label` (form captions), and `TextLink` (anchor sibling of `Text`) cover every
typographic decision in the library. Anything outside the `typography/` folder
that reaches for a font-size class is doing it wrong.

</Section>

<Section title="Inline helpers — HighLight & Underline" id="inline-helpers">

The `HighLight` and `Underline` helpers (from `@/components/typography/typography`)
render small inline accents in four variants — `primary`, `success`,
`warning`, `error`. `DangerousHTML` is the sanitised escape-hatch when you
must render server-trusted HTML.
